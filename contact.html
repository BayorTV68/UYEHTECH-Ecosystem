Digital products deliver instantly — the moment your payment is confirmed, it appears in your dashboard under My Products, ready to download any time. There's no waiting and nothing gets shipped. If a purchase doesn't show up within a few minutes, submit a ticket with your order ID and we'll sort it out fast.

We accept payments through Paystack and Flutterwave, covering cards, bank transfers, and more. Prices can be shown in your preferred currency, though the actual charge settles in the currency displayed at checkout.

Apply through Become a Creator. Once approved, you get your own branded storefront, can list products, and track every sale in a real-time earnings ledger. Payouts can be requested straight from your Creator dashboard once you clear the minimum threshold.

You get a unique referral link. When someone buys after clicking it, you earn a commission automatically — no manual follow-up. Your commission rate depends on your affiliate tier, shown on your dashboard, and is paid out of the creator's share of the sale rather than added on top of the price.

Since products deliver instantly, sales are generally final once delivered.
