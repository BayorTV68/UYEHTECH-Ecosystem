Questions

Frequently asked

Who can become a creator on UYEH TECH?
Anyone can apply — freelancers, developers, designers, educators, and business owners. You need to be 18+ and agree to our terms.
How long does approval take?
We review all applications within 24–48 hours. You'll receive an email with the outcome.
What is the revenue split?
You keep 80% of every sale. UYEH TECH takes 20% as a platform commission. If an affiliate referred the sale, their cut (10%) comes from your 80%.
What types of products can I sell?
Digital products only — templates, UI components, code snippets, courses, e-books, design assets, tools, and more.
When and how do I get paid?
Once your balance reaches $50, you can request a withdrawal via bank transfer, PayPal, Flutterwave, or crypto. Processing takes 3–5 business days.
Can I apply if I already have an account?
Yes! Sign in first then revisit this page. Your existing account will be upgraded to a creator account upon approval.
