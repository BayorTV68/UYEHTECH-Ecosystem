UYEH TECH Agent Agreement — Summary

By applying to become a UYEH TECH Support Agent, you confirm that all information provided in this application is accurate and complete. Knowingly providing false information is grounds for immediate disqualification and potential legal action.

Data Usage: Your personal data, identity documents, and background information will be stored securely and used solely for the purpose of verifying your identity, processing your agent contract, and conducting your duties as a support agent. Your data will never be sold or shared with third parties without your explicit consent.

Background Checks: You consent to UYEH TECH conducting identity verification, reference checks, and where applicable, criminal background screening prior to approval.

Conduct: As an agent, you agree to maintain the highest standards of professionalism, respect customer confidentiality, and uphold the UYEH TECH Code of Conduct at all times.

Confidentiality: All customer data, platform information, and internal processes you encounter in your role are strictly confidential and must not be disclosed to any unauthorised party.

Termination: UYEH TECH reserves the right to terminate any agent's access at any time, with or without cause, in accordance with applicable law.
