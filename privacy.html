1. Information We Collect

What we collect depends on how you use the Marketplace — browsing requires nothing at all; buying, selling, referring, or applying as Support staff each require progressively more.

1.1 Provided Directly by You

  • Account details: full name, email address, password (stored as a secure hash — we never see or store your actual password), phone number
  • Store details, if you're a Creator: store name, description, logo, business/tax information you choose to add
  • Payout details, if you're a Creator or Affiliate: bank account or payout method information, used solely to send you money you've earned
  • Identity verification documents, if you apply to become a Support Agent: government ID (front and back), a selfie for identity matching, and proof of address
  • Messages, files, and images you send through live chat with Support or with UYEH TECH staff
  • Product content, if you're a Creator: files, images, and descriptions you upload for sale
  • Anything you type into a contact form, review, or coupon/order note

1.2 Collected Automatically

  • Device and browser information, IP address, approximate location (used mainly to set a sensible default currency)
  • Pages visited, actions taken, and referral link clicks (for Affiliate tracking)
  • Cookies and local storage data — see Section 4

1.3 From Payment Processors

When you pay for a product, request a payout, or pay a coupon activation fee, our payment processors (Paystack and Flutterwave) handle your card or bank details directly. We receive confirmation of the transaction (amount, status, a transaction reference) but we do not receive or store your full card number, CVV, or banking password at any point.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account, and authenticate you when you log in
  • To process purchases, deliver digital products, and calculate/pay Creator and Affiliate earnings
  • To verify Support Agent applicants before granting them access to the support system
  • To provide customer support, including through live chat
  • To detect and prevent fraud — including affiliate referral abuse, fraudulent coupon use, and payment disputes
  • To send you transactional communications (order confirmations, payout notices, security alerts) — these are not optional and are not "marketing"
  • To send newsletter or promotional emails, only if you've opted in, and always with an unsubscribe option
  • To improve the Marketplace, understand usage patterns, and fix bugs
  • To comply with legal obligations, including tax and financial recordkeeping requirements

4. Automated Decision-Making & Fraud Detection

We use automated systems to help detect fraud and abuse on the Marketplace — for example, unusual referral link activity, suspicious coupon usage patterns, or payment behavior that resembles a chargeback risk. These systems may automatically flag an account or transaction for review, or in some cases temporarily restrict an action (such as a payout) while a flag is investigated by a human. Automated flagging is not the final word: any suspension or termination decision made as a result of a flag is reviewed by a person before it is finalized, and you can always contact Support (Section 18) to ask about or challenge a decision that affected you.

7. Live Chat & Support Data

Conversations you have with Support through the live chat widget — including any images or files you send or receive — are stored as part of your support history. This lets us follow up on unresolved issues and maintain a record of what was discussed. Support Agents can see your chat history and any files shared within it, for the purpose of assisting you. Please avoid sharing passwords or full payment card numbers in chat; our Support staff will never ask you for these.

8. Data Retention

  • Account data: kept for as long as your account is active, and for a limited period after closure to comply with tax, accounting, and dispute-resolution obligations.
  • Transaction and earnings records: retained for as long as required by applicable financial recordkeeping laws.
  • Support chat history: retained to maintain a service record; you may request deletion subject to Section 10.
  • Support Agent applicant identity documents: if your application is approved, retained as part of your staff record for as long as you remain an agent, plus a reasonable period after. If your application is rejected or withdrawn, these documents are deleted within a reasonable period after the decision, unless we're required to retain them longer by law.

9. Security

We use industry-standard measures to protect your data, including encrypted password storage, authenticated and role-restricted API access (so, for example, a buyer's account cannot access another user's dashboard data), and encrypted connections (HTTPS) across the platform. No system is completely immune to risk, and we cannot guarantee absolute security — but we take reasonable, ongoing steps to protect what you share with us and to close gaps when we find them.

10. Data Breach Notification

If a data breach occurs that creates a real risk to your rights or freedoms — for example, unauthorized access to account credentials, payout details, or identity verification documents — we will notify affected users without undue delay, using the contact details on your account, and will describe what happened, what data was involved, and what steps you can take to protect yourself. Where required by the Nigeria Data Protection Act 2023 or other applicable law, we will also notify the relevant regulator.

11. International Data Transfers

Some of our infrastructure providers (payment processors, cloud hosting, analytics) operate servers outside Nigeria. Where your data is transferred internationally, we rely on those providers' own security and compliance standards (including, where applicable, standard contractual clauses or equivalent safeguards) to protect it in transit and at rest.

12. Your Rights

Regardless of where you're located, you can ask us to:

  • Access — get a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct — fix inaccurate or outdated information
  • Delete — request deletion of your account and associated data, subject to the retention obligations in Section 8
  • Object or restrict — object to certain uses, such as marketing emails, analytics tracking, or automated fraud flagging described in Section 4
  • Port — request your data in a portable format

To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the details in Section 18. We will respond within a reasonable timeframe and may need to verify your identity first.

13. Nigerian Data Protection (NDPA)

UYEH TECH is based in Nigeria, and the Marketplace's primary user base is Nigerian. We process personal data in accordance with the Nigeria Data Protection Act 2023 (NDPA) and the regulations of the Nigeria Data Protection Commission (NDPC). Under the NDPA, you have the right to be informed about how your data is processed (this document), to access and correct your data, to withdraw consent where processing is based on consent (such as marketing cookies), and to lodge a complaint with the NDPC if you believe your rights have been violated.

Our legal basis for processing your data under the NDPA is, depending on the activity: performance of a contract (fulfilling a purchase, payout, or Support Agent application), consent (marketing cookies and newsletter emails), legitimate interest (fraud prevention, platform security, analytics), or compliance with a legal obligation (tax and financial recordkeeping). If you have questions about which basis applies to a specific use of your data, contact us using the details in Section 18.

14. GDPR (EU/UK Users)

If you are located in the European Economic Area or the United Kingdom, you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation in addition to those described above, including the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority. Our legal basis for processing your data is, depending on the activity: performance of a contract (fulfilling a purchase or payout), consent (marketing cookies), or legitimate interest (fraud prevention, analytics).

15. CCPA (California Users)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to know what personal information we collect, to request deletion, and to opt out of the "sale" of personal information — we do not sell personal information, so there is nothing to opt out of in that respect. You will not be discriminated against for exercising any privacy right.

16. Children's Privacy

The Marketplace is not directed at, and is not intended for use by, anyone under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If we become aware that we have collected data from someone under 18 without appropriate consent, we will delete it.
