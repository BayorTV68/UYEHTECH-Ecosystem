Marketplace, Blog & Ecosystem — Frequently Asked Questions

Getting Started

What is the UYEH TECH Marketplace?
+

The Marketplace is where verified Creators sell digital products — templates, courses, UI kits, software, and more — and Buyers get instant access the moment payment clears. Affiliates can also earn commission by referring buyers with a personal link. One account can hold more than one of these roles at once.

Do I need an account to browse?
+

No — browsing products, viewing creator stores, and reading the blog don't require an account. You'll need to sign up when you're ready to purchase, sell, or refer.

I forgot my password — what do I do?
+

Use the Forgot Password link on the login page. You'll get a reset link by email — if it doesn't arrive within a few minutes, check spam before contacting support.

Buying on the Marketplace

How do I purchase a product?
+

Add a product to your cart (or buy it directly), check out, and pay with one of our supported payment methods. Your purchase appears in My Products instantly once payment is confirmed.

What payment methods can I use?
+

We accept payments through Paystack and Flutterwave — covering cards, bank transfers, and more, depending on what each processor supports in your region.

Prices show in my local currency — is that what I'll actually be charged?
+

Prices are converted for your convenience so you know roughly what you're paying before checkout. The actual charge settles in the currency shown at the payment step, using the exchange rate in effect at that moment.

Can I get a refund?
+

Since digital products deliver instantly, sales are generally final once delivered. Refunds are considered for:

  • A verified delivery failure (payment succeeded, product never appeared)
  • A product materially different from its listing
  • A confirmed duplicate charge

Submit a support ticket with your order ID if any of these apply.

Can I leave a review on something I bought?
+

Yes — buyers with a verified purchase can leave a rating and written review. Reviews should reflect your genuine experience with the product.

Selling as a Creator

How do I become a Creator?
+

Apply through Become a Creator. Approval isn't automatic — we review every application before granting access to a storefront.

What can I sell, and what's not allowed?
+

Any digital product you have the legal right to sell — templates, courses, UI kits, ebooks, software, and similar. Every listing is reviewed before going live; we don't allow content that infringes someone else's IP, contains malware, or is misleading about what's actually included.

How much commission does the platform take?
+

Your exact rate is shown on your Creator dashboard. Where a sale comes through an affiliate's referral link, that affiliate's commission is paid out of your share of the sale — it's never added on top of the platform's own cut, so the platform's percentage never changes based on whether a sale was referred.

How and when do I get paid?
+

Confirmed earnings become available once they clear any holding period. Request a payout any time you're at or above the minimum threshold shown on your dashboard, in any currency you hold a balance in. Payouts typically complete within a few business days.

Can I run discount coupons on my products?
+

Yes. Creating a coupon involves a small platform fee based on the coupon's total discount value, paid upfront before the coupon goes live. Full details are shown when you create one from your dashboard.

The Affiliate Program

How does the Affiliate program work?
+

You get a unique referral link. When someone buys after clicking it (within the tracked window), you earn commission automatically — no manual follow-up or invoicing needed.

How much do affiliates earn, and does it change over time?
+

You're placed into a tier based on your total referral count, and higher tiers earn a higher commission percentage. Your current tier, rate, and progress toward the next tier are all shown on your Affiliate dashboard.

I just signed up as an affiliate — why isn't my first referral earning anything?
+

New affiliates need to reach a minimum referral count before commission starts being credited — this threshold is shown on your dashboard. It's a fraud-prevention measure; once you clear it, every qualifying referral (including ones already tracked) earns normally.

The UYEH TECH Blog

What's on the UYEH TECH Blog?
+

Articles on web development, the creator economy, building an online income, and updates about the Marketplace itself — practical, not just promotional.

Can I write a guest post or suggest a topic?
+

Yes — send a short pitch through our contact page (select General Enquiry) with an outline of what you'd like to cover.

How do I get notified about new posts?
+

Subscribe through the newsletter form on the homepage or blog — you'll get an email confirmation to click before you're fully subscribed (this keeps the list genuine and spam-free).

Support & Trust

How do I contact support, and how fast will I hear back?
+

Use live chat for the fastest response (usually under 5 minutes during business hours), or submit a support ticket for anything that needs detail or attachments — tickets are typically answered within 24 hours.

I already submitted a ticket — how do I check on it?
+

On the Contact & Support page, switch to the Track Existing tab and enter your Ticket ID with the email you used to submit it.

Is my payment information safe?
+

Payments are handled directly by Paystack and Flutterwave — we never see or store your full card number or banking password. See our Privacy Policy for the complete breakdown of what we do and don't collect.

What if I suspect a scam listing or fraudulent activity?
+

Report it immediately through a support ticket with as much detail as possible (product link, order ID, screenshots). We investigate every report and take action, including removing listings and suspending accounts, where our policies are violated.
